Tích hợp liên tục (Continuous Integration, viết tắt CI) là kĩ thuật hợp nhất thường xuyên mã nguồn từ những nhà phát triển riêng lẻ vào một nhánh chính duy nhất.

background-repeat: repeat (default)

Here, the background image is repeated both vertically and horizontally. The last image will be clipped if it does not fit. This is default:

Hello World!

background-repeat: space

Here, the background-image is repeated as much as possible without clipping. The first and last images are pinned to either side of the element, and whitespace is distributed evenly between the images.

Hello World!

background-repeat: round

Here, the background-image is repeated and squished or stretched, to fill the space (no gaps):
